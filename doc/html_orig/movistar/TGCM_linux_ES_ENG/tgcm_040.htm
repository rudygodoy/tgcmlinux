Using Escritorio Movistar

 

«« BackNext »»



 

How to start up Escritorio Movistar

You can start up Escritorio Movistar in two ways:

  • From the GUI, you have a shortcut to the application. Click on Applications menu, then click on Internet >> Escritorio Movistar.

  • If you use Ubuntu or Fedora (Unity shell), click on Dash home or Activities, type escritorio movistar, and then click on Escritorio Movistar icon.

Figure 2. Escritorio Movistar application icon.

 

  • From the command line, open a Terminal and enter in the command line tgcm -c es and then press Enter.

Every time you insert your Mobile Internet Device, you will prompt to indicate what type of user you are, Prepaid or Postpaid. This window will not show you once you've entered the required data or press OK button, located at bottom of the window.

Escritorio Movistar also includes multilanguage support (spanish and english). To change the language, from the task bar, go to System >> Administration >> Language Support. Close Escritorio Movistar application and reopen it to check that the language has changed. Note that the language change has a global effect on the whole system, which is now displayed in the language you have chosen.

It is necessary for your Linux distribution has the Gnome desktop installed for the proper works of the Escritorio Movistar application.

For statistical purposes, Telefónica Móviles España, S.A.U, processes anonymous user details related to the use of Escritorio Movistar services, connection details, operating system and software versions, for the sole aim of identifying possible improvements in the services and enhancing user-friendliness. No personal details are processed under any circumstances.

 

 

Composition of the Graphic Interface

Escritorio Movistar main window is composed of three different zones:

Figure 3. Escritorio Movistar main window.

 

  • General Zone. This zone shows the device used, traffic data meter with the used data and Settings and Help buttons.
  • Services Zone: Here you can access all the available services and features offered by Escritorio Movistar.
  • Connectivity Zone: Here you can connect and check information on the active connection.

A series of popup messages will appear whenever something happens; for example, whether you receive a SMS, or whether there has been a problem establishing the connection or whether it has ended unexpectedly.

 

 

General Zone

Figure 4. General zone detail


General zone is placed on top of main window of Escritorio Movistar; from here you can to access to general options. Into General zone, there are distinct zones: Device zone, Traffic data meter and Settings and Help buttons.

  • Device zone: in this place, you can see a button with the device brand, an icon that shows the GPRS/EDGE/3G/3.5G/3.5G+ or HSPA+ coverage level and the operator. If you are in roaming mode, you can see an icon triangle.

By clicking on the button with the device brand, a contextual menu shall appear. This menu shall offer you the following options:

- Deactivate/Activate PIN: Here you can decide whether the program should request the PIN code for your SIM card or not, every time you connect. To do this you simply need to enter your current PIN.

- Change PIN: To change the PIN code on your SIM card you simply enter the old PIN for your SIM card and then you also enter the new PIN that you want to set up.

- Network technology selection: The option we recommend the most, which is set up by default, is Automatic, but if you want you can program Escritorio Movistar to only connect to the type of network you want.

- Carrier selection: Escritorio Movistar automatically connects to the Movistar network, but you can select the carrier you are going to connect with, pressing  Manual. After performing the search, you simply choose the carrier you want from the list the program provides and press Select.

- Turn on/off the device.

  • Traffic data meter: In this zone you can see your data volume used. Check Traffic section for further information.
  • Settings: Here you can configure all the options of Escritorio Movistar application. See Settings  section for further information.
  • Help: Here you can see what version of the application you are using, and get help on how to use Escritorio Movistar correctly. See the Help section in this document.

 

Services Zone

Service zone is located on a horizontal strip on the right and contains icons that give access to the services provided by Escritorio Movistar.

Figure 5. Services zone detail.

 

  • Top-Up: If you are a Prepaid Customer, and if you have selected this option in the My details tab, Top-Up service will be activated and will be visible from Escritorio Movistar main window.
  • Wi-Fi Zone. With Wi-Fi Zone service you can connect to Movistar Wi-Fi Hotspot for free and with no limits, according your rate.
  • SMS. You can send and receive SMS from your computer with the SMS service. This service is available only if you connect through a Mobile Internet Device.
  • Contacts. This service allows you to save and view information about your contacts, whether they can import from other sources as well as contacts that have been registered with Escritorio Movistar.
  • Favorites. Favorites are a quick way of finding and using your Internet address or favorite applications in Escritorio Movistar.
  • Intranet. This service allows you to connect to your company’s corporate Intranet. By default, the service is not configured; you need to contact your network administrator to obtain the connection information.

Clicking on More button you can access to additional services offered by Escritorio Movistar.

 

Conectivity Zone

Figure 6. Connectivity zone detail.


In this zone the application window shows tags with general information on the connection you are using. This information is updated automatically. The following data is shown:

  • Connection. Once connected, shows the name of the active connection you are now using to connect to Escritorio Movistar, its status (connected o disconnected), and the strength of the signal and its speed.
  • Available networks. By clicking on this button, a list of the available Wi-Fi and WWAN networks shall be shown. The signal quality is represented by an icon to the left. If it's a Wi-Fi, to the right is an icon indicating their level of safety (secure network or unprotected network). Select the network you want to connect and then click on Connect button.
  • Connect. By clicking on Connect button you can establish a connection through the smart connector manager, according to the order established. Check My Internet connections for further information.

Escritorio Movistar lets you connect to through different types of connections:

  • WWAN: Allows you connect to different Movistar network technologies (GPRS/EDGE/3G/3.5G/3.5G+ or HSPA+).
  • Wi-Fi: You can connect to any of the Wi-Fi networks within reach at the time. You can use Mobile Internet Devices with this functionality built-in or the Wi-Fi technology on your computer.
  • LAN: Detects if you have an IP address assigned and it is used to access to the services of Escritorio Movistar. If the application finds that a LAN connection is available, this will let you to connect through a LAN wherever possible.

For more information on how Create or Delete connections or change their order of preference, see My Internet connections section.


Popup Messages

When you have a Mobile Internet Device selected as connecting device, and is properly registered on the network, Escritorio Movistar will display a popups at the bottom reporting you of certain events, such as:

  • Incoming SMS
  • Carrier messages (e.g. billing period, credit expired).
  • If a problem is detected when the connection is lost or if it unexpectedly.

In some cases, these messages you receive will be clickable and doing click on them with the mouse will open an Escritorio Movistar service or directly opens the Internet browser.


Keyboard Shortcuts

Below we detail keyboard shortcut defined for Escritorio Movistar software, to improve accessibility for handicapped people.

  • Connect  -> CTRL+C
  • Disconnect  -> CTRL+D
  • Exit Escritorio Movistar -> CTRL+Q

These keyboard shortcuts are functional when the Escritorio Movistar main window is selected.
