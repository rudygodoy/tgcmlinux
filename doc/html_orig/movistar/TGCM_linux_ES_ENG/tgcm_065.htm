Traffic

 

«« BackNext »»



By clicking on the Traffic zone, located in Escritorio Movistar main window upper zone, Traffic window will open.

Figure 9. Traffic zone detail.

 

Traffic window contains two tabs: Current Period and History.

Figure 10. Traffic.

 

The Current Period tab informs you on the speed (in Kbits/s), connection time (in minutes) and the traffic data that was sent and received and the connection time (in minutes). In the same tab, you can see the Current Billing Period section which shows information on the total Data transferred in the current billing period. It also indicates the number of SMS sent. It shows information about data used in roaming traffic as well.

You can choose the day you start your billing cycle by clicking Change your billing period. This selection can also do it by clicking from Settings >> Options >> My details.

History tab shows graphically the total data transferred in the last 6 months, national and roaming, and the transference data limit. Note that the History is associated to the SIM card therefore you will only see the accumulated data traffic for the specific SIM card you are using at a particular moment.

Take into account that the counters for transferred traffic per connection, maximum speed and connection time are automatically set to zero when you disconnect. Press Reset button to remove the accumulated traffic information.

The traffic measurement is approximate and it is a mere estimation of the amount of data transferred. You can consult your billing data at Canal Cliente.
