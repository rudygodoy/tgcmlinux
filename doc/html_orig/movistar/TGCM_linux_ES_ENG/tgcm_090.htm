How do I navigate through Internet using Escritorio Movistar

  1. Click on Escritorio Movistar desktop icon to open the application.

  2. Connect your Mobile Internet Device to the computer.

  3. Now it will ask you for your PIN code from your SIM card (if it is activated); after you enter it, click on Accept. Wait for the network coverage indicator to turn green.

  4. Click on Available Networks button and select the WWAN or Wi-Fi network that you want to connect to and click on Connect button.

  5. Open your Internet browser.

If you are having difficulties with this process, you can go to the Troubleshooting section for help.

 

How do I send a SMS using Escritorio Movistar

  1. Click on Escritorio Movistar desktop icon to open the application.

  2. Connect your Mobile Internet Device to the computer.

  3. Now it will ask you for your PIN code from your SIM card (if it is activated); after you enter it, click on Accept. Wait for the network coverage indicator to turn green.

  4. Select SMS icon in the services zone and type in the details to send the message. If you have any questions refer to the SMS section of this document.

If you are having difficulties with this process, you can go to the Troubleshooting section for help.

 

How do I send a SMS through the web using Escritorio Movistar

  1. Click on Escritorio Movistar desktop icon to open the application.

  2. Connect your Mobile Internet Device to the computer.

  3. Now it will ask you for your PIN code from your SIM card (if it is activated); after you enter it, click on Accept. Wait for the network coverage indicator to turn green.

  4. Click on Available Networks button and select the WWAN or Wi-Fi network what you want to connect and click on Connect button.

  5. Click on Mensajería Web from the Favourites service. If this is the first time you access the service, you need to enter your telephone number and password. Once you have accessed the web page, select the Access button.

Note that if you are not yet a Movistar services user you have forgotten your password, use your mobile to request your password by sending a free SMS with the password you want to 22770. For example, send the word “Blues” to 22770 and “Blues” will be your password.

If you are having difficulties with this process, you can go to the Troubleshooting section for help. To configure the Mensajería Web, go to the User Details section.
