Changing Settings

 

How Connection Manager works

  • Home. Connection Manager is set to start up automatically when you start up your computer. To change this -so you start it yourself- untick Launch Connection Manager when I start my computer.
  • Auto connect. Connection Manager is set to automatically connect to the Internet when you start up your computer. To change these options, untick Connect me automatically when application starts-up.
  • Updates and News. Connection Manager checks regularly for the latest news or updates from O2.
    • To switch off auto updates: Untick Automatically download recent news and updates when connecting.

    • To see the lastest updates and news: Click on See news and updates. To install an update, highlight it and click Install.

 

How Connection Manager looks

  • Change the main menu. You can use the menú to get to all of Connection Manager's extra apps (like Text messages, Wi-Fi and Favourites). You can also choose which services appear on the menu, in what order.

    1. Click Settings, then Display.

    2. Select the apps you want to see.

    3. Use the up or down buttons to change the order around.

 

  • Extra apps. If you want to, you can Uninstall the extra apps - texts, Wi-Fi directory or Favourites.
    • To uninstall an app:

      1. Click Settings, then Services.

      2. Find the app you want to remove, untick the box next to the service.

      3. The app is automatically uninstalled.

 

You Account Details

To see or change your account details:

  1. Go to Settings, then General.
  1. Click on the My details tab.

  1. Enter the right details for you contract: Pay and Go or Pay Monthly,  Monthly download allowance, Billing date, Mobile Broadband Number and Mobile Phone Number.
  1. Click OK to save data.

 

Your PIN settings

You can turn on, turn off or change your PIN. Click Settings, then General, then Networks and SIM card tab.

  • To turn off your PIN: Tick the Desactivate PIN box.

  • To turn on your PIN: Untick the Desactivate PIN box.
  • To Change your PIN:
    • Click Change PIN

    • Type in your old PIN, then Confirm new PIN.

    • Click Accept to save your changes.

     

Your Network settings

Connection Manager is set to connect to the fastest available network: first 3G, then GPRS. And always O2, if possible.
We recommend you leave these settings as they are. Only changes them if you know what you're doing. (Or if you're got an IT professional to hand).
Choosing a network. To set how Connection Manager connects to cellular networks.

  1. Click Settings, then General.
  1. Click on the Networks and SIM card tab.

  1. Under Network technology selection mode, click Manual.

  1. Choose the kind of network you want from the drop-down list.
  1. Click OK to save changes.

Going abroad (operator selection). If you go abroad Connection Manager will try to connect to the local O2 network, or one of our partners, automatically. You don't need to change anything.

But if you want to choose a different network, you can. Again, we recommend you have these settings as they are, unless you know what you're doing.

  1. Click Settings, then General.
  1. Click on the Networks and SIM card tab.
  1. Under Network Selection, click Manual, then click Search.

  1. Choose the network you want from the drop-down list.
  1. Click Save to save changes.

 

Internet connections settings

You can add new3G or GPRS connections manually.


  To add or change a 3G/GPRS connection

  1. Click Settings, then My Internet connections.

  1. Click New GPRS/3G connection.

  1. Fill in all of the settings for your connection; if you don’t know the settings, ask your network manager.
  1. Click Save to save your changes.

 

 Change an existing connection

To change the details for a connection you've already set up:

  1. Click Settings, then My Internet connections.

  1. Highlight the connection you want to change from the list. Click Change.

  1. Fill in all of the settings for your connection; if you don’t know the settings, ask your network manager.
  1. Click Save to save your changes.

 

 How to export connections

To export a connection, access Settings, then My Internet connections, select a connection from the list and press the Export button. Then enter the name and place in which you want to save the file and press Save. This will generate a file that you can subsequently recover at any time.

 How to import connections

Once you have access Settings, then My Internet connections, press the Import button and a new window will open so that you can select the place that the file you want to import is located. You can also import by directly opening the file with a .tgcm extension. This file will hold the information needed to configure the Connection Manager connection.

 

Add new devices

Connection Manager is already set up to work with your O2 dongle. And it should recognise any modems you’ve already installed on your computer.
If you have another dongle modem that you want to use, you might need to install or select it.

 Install a dongle (3G/GPRS)

  • Insert you dongle into a free USB 2.0 port in your computer and it will be recognized automatically.

 

 To use a different dongle

  1. Click Settings, then Devices.
  1. Highlight the name of the dongle you want to use. Then click on it.

 

Your text settings

When you send texts from Connection Manager, it’ll automatically send them from the O2 Message Centre.
To send texts from a different message centre:

  1. Click Settings, then Services.
  1. Click Text and Type in the message centre number, then tick Use this short message centre box and type the right data.
