Keep track of what you use

 

On Pay Monthly, Connection Manager keeps track of how much of your monthly download allowance you’ve used. To get a more detailed view of what you’ve used, click on You’ve used…

In You’ve used window you can see next information:

More details

  • This session: how long you’ve been online since you last connected.
  • How Much: how much data you’ve downloaded since you last connected (Note that this counter includes your Wi-Fi usage as well as usage from your bundle on the O2 network).
  • Monthly allowance: your monthly usage allowance on the O2 network.
  • What you’ve used: how much you’ve used in total this billing period.
  • What’s left: how much you’ve got left this billing period.
  • Data Abroad: how much you’ve used abroad this billing period.
  • Texts sent: how many texts you’ve sent this billing period.

 

 Change your data allowance details

If your data allowance or bill date is wrong, click on My Details to change it.

 

 Turn on data allowance alerts

If you’re on Pay Monthly, Connection Manager can send you an alert as a pop-up message, when you’re getting close to your monthly limit. Here’s how:


1. Check your data allowance details are correct (see Change your data allowance details, above).


2. Click Settings, then Alerts.


3. Choose when you want a reminder (when you hit 50%, or 75% of your allowance, for example).


You'll get a pop-up message when you reach that limit.

 

 Change your billing date details

If your billing date is wrong, click My details to change it. See Your account details for more.

 

 What you’ve used – a history

To see what you’ve used each month since you joined, click on the History tab.
