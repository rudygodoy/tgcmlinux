Getting started

 

Connecting to Internet

When you open Connection Manager, you'll see this screen


  • View usage and buy access (Pay and Go customers only): Clicking on this link when you’re connected will allow you to check how much credit you have left and to buy more if you need it. 

  • What you’ve used (Pay Monthly users only): This shows you how much of your monthly download allowance you’ve used and how much is left.

You need to tell Connection Manager what your allowance is, first. See the  section called  My details for more.

  • Status: This shows you if you’re connected, trying to connect or disconnected from the internet.
  • Connect: Click this to connect to a network near you.
  • Available networks: Click this to see a list of networks you can log on to.
  • Find Hotspot: Type in where you are, or where you’re going. Connection Manager will find a Wi-Fi hotspot nearby.
  • Texts: Send or receive texts.
  • Settings: Change your settings. (See Changing settings section for more).

When you click on More... icon, you can see more services and features available for Connection Manager. To see the icons on contextual menus, you can use gconf-editor application and change to a "true" value next path: /desktop/gnome/interface/menus_have_icons
