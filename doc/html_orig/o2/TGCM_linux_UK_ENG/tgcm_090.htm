Common Problems

 

 If you’ve got an old Operating System


Connection Manager needs an up-to-date operating system to work properly. Use Software Update service to get the latest updates for your operating system.

 If other programs are stopping Connection Manager


If you've got anti-virus software, firewalls or others programs to do with your mobile or internet connections, the might not let you install Connection Manager. Try turning them off temporarily while you're installing or adjusting your settings.

 If Connection Manager can’t fin your dongle

Try this:

  • Unplug your dongle from your computer’s USB socket.
  • Restart your computer.
  • If Connection Manager opens, close it.
  • Plug your dongle back and wait for your computer recognize it.
  • Open Connection Manager again.

 If your dongle isn’t lighting up

Check:

  • Your dongle´s plugged in tightly.
  • Your dongle´s turned on.
  • Your dongle works in a different USB socket on your computer.
  • Your dongle works in another computer.

 If you can’t connect to the Internet

  • Check your dongle's plugged in tight.
  • Check you dongle's on.
  • In Connection Manager, go to Settings , then My Internet connections.
  • Click Modify, then choose Default.
