Before you start...

 


About Connection Manager

Connection Manager connects you to the Internet, using the fastest connection available, wherever you are. From a desktop computer or a laptop.

You can also use it to:

  • Send Texts from your computer.
  • Keep track of your Contacts.
  • Set up your Favourites, so you go straight to your favourite websites or programs.
  • Find your nearest Wi-Fi Hotspot.

 

What you need

To use Connection Manager, you need an O2 Mobile Broadband account. You’ll also need:

  • A computer with:

    • USB 2.0 socket.
    • Linux OS - Supported distros: Ubuntu 10.10, Fedora 14 and OpenSuse 11.4
    • 32-bit and 64-bit full support.
  • An O2 Mobile Broadband dongle.
  • An O2 sim (to go in your dongle).

 

Tips

  • Pop-ups

Along the way, you might get pop-up messages on your screen. These messages will give you useful information about connection manager or your connection. You can close and ignore them once you’ve read them.

  • Change settings

On some screens, you’ll see a Settings button. Click on it to change the settings for whatever part of Connection Manager you’re using.

  • Help

If you need help at any time, click the [?] icon on your screen (It’s usually in the top right-hand corner).
