Installing the hardware

 

«« BackNext »»



Before installing and configuring your Mobile Internet Device, make sure that you have installed the O2 Průvodce připojením software correctly. This process is described in Installing O2 Průvodce připojením software section.

 

Requirements for Installation

To install the application, you need the following:

  • Computer compatible with USB 2.0 port free.
  • Mobile Internet Device.
  • SIM card activated in the Movistar GPRS/3G service.

 

 

Mobile Internet Device

Once the software has been installed, and whenever you start up O2 Průvodce připojením, the operating system will automatically detect the insertion of a Mobile Internet Device in your computer.

Do the following to install the Mobile Internet Device in your computer:

  1. First, insert the O2 SIM card into your Mobile Internet Device.

The way to insert the SIM card in the Mobile Internet Device, and the color and status of their LEDs, may vary according to the model in question.

  1. No specific configuration is needed before insertion for Internet Mobile Devices. Simply insert the Modem and they will be automatically recognized by the system and the application, as long as the drivers for these devices were installed when you installed the program.
  1. The start-up process will begin after the Mobile Internet Device is inserted. The LEDs on the device will light up to indicate that the process is started (note that the LED color may vary depending on the model of the Mobile Internet Device).
  1. After starting up the mobile Internet card or modem, you need to register the SIM card on the O2 network. To do this, enter your code PIN for your SIM card when the system prompts you to do so.

You only have three tries to correctly enter the code PIN for your SIM card, so take care when entering the PIN numbers.
